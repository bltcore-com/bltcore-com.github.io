# blt-core

### Sometimes People Ask 

Here are a few other things people sometimes ask about BLT Core, Beyond Limited Thinking, and this site:

#### What is BLT Core actually used for?
BLT Core is a modular, AI-centric point-of-sale (POS) and retail infrastructure. It's designed for modern restaurants and retail, offering open-standards, zero lock-in, and rapid, flexible integration with payment processors, banks, ISOs, and partners.

#### Is BLT a POS product or a software company?
Both: BLT (Beyond Limited Thinking, LLC) is a software company. BLT Core is its flagship product: a POS platform emphasizing flexibility, control, and "payments freedom" for merchants and ISOs.

#### Do you make your own hardware?
No. BLT Core is software-first: it runs anywhere, and works with standard devices. No proprietary hardware requirements.

#### Why is "Beyond Limited Thinking" the company name?
Because we believe true innovation in payments and POS requires going *beyond* the assumptions and restrictions of legacy systems, unlocking new ways to benefit merchants and partners.

#### What makes BLT Core different?
- Payments-agnostic—works with many processors, no lock-in
- AI-native workflows: automation, smart setup, recommendation, and integration
- Modular, scriptable, open standards
- Data and schema transparency
- Designed for rapid prototyping and composability

#### Who is your platform for?
- Banks, ISOs, fintech partners, and forward-thinking restaurant/retail operators
- Anyone who values ownership of their data and payment flows
